Privacy Policy for WeekLisk

Effective Date: April 26, 2026

Thank you for choosing WeekLisk. This Privacy Policy helps you understand what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it. Our primary goal is to provide you with a powerful tool to manage your weekly goals while respecting your privacy to the fullest extent.

1. Information Collection and Use

WeekLisk is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not operate any external servers to collect, store, or process your personal information.

  • Local Storage: All data you enter—including your weekly goals, sub-tasks, and history—is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. We have no access to this data.
  • Local Notifications: WeekLisk utilizes Apple's Local Notification framework for weekly reminders. This happens entirely on your device.

2. Third-Party Services and APIs

To provide personalized podcast recommendations, WeekLisk communicates with a third-party service:

  • Apple iTunes Search API: The titles of your goals are sent as search queries directly to Apple's iTunes Search API (itunes.apple.com). No personally identifiable information (such as your name or device ID) is attached. This data exchange is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Security

Since your goals are stored locally on your device, their security is inherently tied to the security of your iOS device. We recommend using Face ID, Touch ID, or a strong passcode to protect your device.
